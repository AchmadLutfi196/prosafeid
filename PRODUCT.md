# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
HR & HSE Managers, corporate safety officers, and individual professionals seeking K3 safety certifications (Kemnaker/BNSP) in Indonesia.

## Product Purpose
Provide a high-fidelity, high-converting interface to explore K3 training schedules, discover corporate solutions, verify certificate authenticity, and seamlessly submit inquiries.

## Brand Personality
Professional Authority, Safety-First Trust, Expert Confidence

## Anti-references
Cluttered governmental portals, outdated static directories, low-contrast forms, and generic templates.

## Design Principles
- Safety-First Authority: Clean, structured layouts that project compliance and security.
- Frictionless Conversion: Prominent, clear CTAs and easy-to-use forms.
- Transparent Verifiability: Instantly search and verify schedules, locations, and certificate credentials.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA compliance, robust typographic hierarchy, high color contrast, and seamless mobile responsiveness.
